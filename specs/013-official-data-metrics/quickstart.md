# Quickstart: Official Data Metrics and Scores

## 📊 Viewing Global Metrics
1. Start the backend and frontend servers.
2. Navigate to the **Base Oficial** tab on the left sidebar.
3. Observe the top of the page. You should see prominent "Big Numbers" indicating the total number of registered accounting firms and the sum of all evaluated interactions.

## 📝 Auditing Interaction Scores
1. In the **Base Oficial** tab, select any accounting firm to view its interactions.
2. In the interaction table (`AtendimentoPicker`), look at the columns on the right.
3. For interactions that have already been evaluated, you will see specific scores for:
   - **Média**
   - **Comunicação**
   - **Profissionalismo**
   - **Resolução**
4. For interactions without an evaluation, these columns will display a dash (`-`).
