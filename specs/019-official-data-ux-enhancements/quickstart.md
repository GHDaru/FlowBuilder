# Quickstart: Official Data UX Enhancements

## 🏢 Multi-Firm Selection
1. Open the **Base Oficial** tab.
2. Observe that all firms in the list are checked by default.
3. Use the checkboxes to select or deselect specific firms.
4. Click the **Avaliados** header to sort the list by the number of evaluations.
5. Click **Ver Atendimentos** (button appearing when selection > 0).

## 📊 Interaction Audit
1. In the combined interaction view, verify that all interactions from all selected firms are displayed.
2. Observe that all rows are selected by default.
3. Click the checkbox in the table header to **Deselect All** or **Select All**.
4. Click on the **Média** header to find the lowest-scoring interactions quickly.
5. Verify that sorting works for **Comunicação**, **Profissionalismo**, and **Resolução** as well.
